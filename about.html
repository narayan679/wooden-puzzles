Origins and Evolution

Wooden Puzzle History

1762

John Spilsbury introduces the first jigsaw puzzle by mounting a map onto wood, creating an educational tool for geography lessons.

1800s

Jigsaw puzzles gain popularity among both adults and children, evolving from educational aids to entertainment.
